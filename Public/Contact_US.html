Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TallyTrack Africa Awards

How do I vote for my favorite nominee?

To vote for your favorite nominee, simply navigate to the Nominees section, select the category, find the nominee you wish to vote for, and click the "Vote Now" button. You'll need to be registered and logged in to cast your vote.

Can I vote multiple times?

No, to ensure fairness, each registered user can vote only once per category. However, you can vote in multiple different categories.

How are nominees selected?

Nominees are selected through a rigorous process that includes public nominations, industry expert recommendations, and our internal research team's evaluation based on achievement, impact, and excellence in their respective fields.

When will the voting period end?

The voting period typically ends 14 days before the awards ceremony. Exact dates are announced on our website and social media channels. You can also check the countdown timer on the voting page.

How can I become a nominee?

Nominations for most categories are open to the public during specific nomination periods. You can submit nominations through our website or be recommended by industry experts. Some categories may have specific eligibility criteria.

Is the voting process secure and transparent?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and verification systems to ensure the integrity of the voting process. Our system prevents duplicate voting and maintains transparency through real-time result tracking (visible after voting closes).

How are the winners determined?

Winners are determined by a combination of public votes (70% weight) and judges' scores (30% weight). Our panel of judges consists of industry experts who evaluate nominees based on specific criteria for each category.

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, once a vote is submitted, it cannot be changed. We recommend reviewing your selection carefully before confirming your vote.

How can I attend the awards ceremony?

Information about attending the awards ceremony, including ticket sales and venue details, will be announced on our website and social media channels approximately one month before the event. Some nominees and sponsors may receive complimentary tickets.

Who can I contact for partnership opportunities?

For partnership, sponsorship, or media collaboration opportunities, please email partnerships@tallytrackafrica.com or use the contact form above and select "Partnership Opportunities" as your enquiry topic.
