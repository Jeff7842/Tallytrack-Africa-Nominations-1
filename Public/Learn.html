How To Vote

Follow these simple steps to cast your vote for your favorite nominees. Your participation helps recognize and celebrate excellence across Africa.

Start Voting Now
01

Access the Dashboard

From the homepage, click on the "Dashboard" link in the navigation menu at the top of the screen. This will take you to the voting dashboard where you can view all categories and nominees.

02

Select a Category

Browse through the categories listed on the sidebar. Click on your desired category to view all nominees in that category. You can also use the "All Categories" option to see nominees across all categories.

03

Choose a Nominee

Browse through the nominees in your selected category. Each nominee card displays their name, photo, and current vote count. When you find your preferred nominee, click the "Vote Now" button on their card.

04

Complete Voting Details

A voting modal will appear with the nominee's information pre-filled. Enter your M-Pesa phone number and select the number of votes you wish to purchase. The total cost will be calculated automatically.

05

M-Pesa Payment

After clicking "Proceed to Pay", you will receive an STK push notification on your phone. Open your M-Pesa menu and enter your PIN to authorize the payment. The system will automatically verify your payment.

06

Payment Confirmation

Once your payment is successfully processed, you will see a confirmation message. If the payment fails, you'll be prompted to try again. All transactions are secure and processed through Safaricom's M-Pesa API.

07

Votes Updated

The nominee's vote count will be updated automatically in real-time. You can see the updated totals on the nominee's card. All votes are recorded transparently in our system to ensure a fair voting process.
