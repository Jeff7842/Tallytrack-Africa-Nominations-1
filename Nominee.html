Nomination Process

How To Become A Nominee

Being nominated for a TallyTrack Africa Award is a prestigious honor that recognizes excellence and achievement across various fields. Our rigorous selection process ensures that only the most deserving individuals are featured on our platform.

1

Submit Your Application

Fill out the nomination form with your details, achievements, and supporting information.

2

Review Process

Our committee will review your application within 24-72 hours to verify eligibility.

3

Approval Notification

If approved, you'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

4

Profile Activation

Your nominee profile will be activated, and you'll be eligible for voting in your category.
